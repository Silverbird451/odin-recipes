Potato Meat Pie

A pie made from smashed potatoes and minced meat

This recipe is delicious simple to make and rich in proteins that come from the meat and carbohydrates, potassium and vitamin c from the potato, along with healthy fats coming from the milk, olive or butter.

Ingredients:

  • Minced Meat
  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Parsley
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps:

  1. Cut the onions and the red bell pepper into small pieces.
  2. Put some olive oil into a sauce pan and then put the chopped onions and red bell pepper into it.
  3. Constantly stir the mix so your onions will not get burned, until the onion get a yellowish color.
  4. Add your minced meat into the sauce pan along with some chopped parsley.
  5. Constantly stir until the meat is cooked.
  6. Cut potatoes into small pieces and then boil them until they are soft, add some salt to the water.
  7. Take potatoes out of the water and throw some milk and butter on to them, and them smash the potatoes.
  8. Put the minced meat on the bottom of a cooking tray and then add the smashed potatoes on top.
  9. Put the tray in to the oven and bake it for 20 minutes and then take it out.
  10. Eat it.
