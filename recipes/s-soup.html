Squash Soup

A soup made from squash

A simple and delicious meal to have specially in the cold winter season. Squash is rich in vitamin C, Magnesium, Calcium, Iron, Vitamine A, Vitamine B6

Ingredients:

  • Squash
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Parsley
  • Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Basil

Steps:

  1. Cut the onion and the garlic into small pieces
  2. Add olive oil into a saucepan and throw the onion and garlic into it.
  3. Cook the onions and the garlic until the onions get a yellowish color, remeber to constantly stir.
  4. Peel and cut the squash int to pieces.
  5. When the onions are ready add the squash pieces into the saucepan along with some salt and sufficient water to cover everything inside.
  6. Add parsley, pepper, oregano and basil.
  7. Cook the mix until the squash pieces are soft.
  8. Take an electric handblender and make the mix into an homogeneous orange liquid.
  9. Eat it.
